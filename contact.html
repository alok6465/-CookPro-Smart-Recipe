Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I save recipes?

Click the bookmark icon on any recipe card or use the "Save" button in the recipe modal. You need to be logged in to save recipes.

How does AI recipe matching work?

Our AI analyzes your ingredients and matches them with recipes in our database using fuzzy search algorithms for the best results.

What are Premium features?

Premium features include PDF downloads, meal planning, nutrition tracking, voice commands, and exclusive recipes from celebrity chefs.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.

Can I submit my own recipes?

Yes! Use the contact form above to submit your family recipes. We review all submissions and may feature them on our platform.
